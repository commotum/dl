In this topic, we'll learn how to carefully interpret descriptions of physical phenomena and translate them into precise mathematical formulas. This skill is essential in physics and engineering because definitions of new quantities are often stated in words, and our job is to recognize the structure and set up the correct formula corresponding to the description.

Let's start by considering a concrete example:
