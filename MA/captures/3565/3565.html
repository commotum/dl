Circular Segments and Sectors
?

Finally, we have two more elements of a circle to remember:

  • A circular sector is the region bounded by an arc of the circle and two radii which connect the center of the circle with the endpoints of the arc. In the left diagram below, we have the circular sector

  • A circular segment is the region bounded by an arc of the circle and the chord that connects the endpoints of the arc. In the right diagram below, we have the circular segment
