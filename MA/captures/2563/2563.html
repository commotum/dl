Introduction

Remember that when items are sold or purchased, the final bill is not always the same as the original price of the item.

  • When there is a markup, the final bill is greater than the original price because some extra amount is added to the original price.

  • When there is a markdown, the final bill is less than the original price because some extra amount is subtracted from the original price.
