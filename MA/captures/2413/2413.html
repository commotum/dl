Example: Identifying True Statements
?

Linda took a walk around Paris. She started at the Eiffel Tower, and she walked for hours. Using a GPS navigator, she recorded her distance from the Eiffel Tower after every hour. The results are shown below.

Which of the following statements are true regarding Linda's walk?

  1. After hours, her distance from the Eiffel Tower was kilometers.
  2. After hours, she was at her maximum distance from the Eiffel Tower.
  3. Her distance from the Eiffel Tower after hour and after hours was the same.
EXPLANATION

Let's analyze each statement in turn.

  • From the graph, the distance to the Eiffel Tower after hours was kilometers, not kilometers. So, statement I is false.
  • From the graph, the largest distance from the Eiffel Tower was after hours, and this distance was kilometers. So, statement II is true.
  • From the graph, the distance to the Eiffel tower after hour and after hours was kilometers. Therefore, statement III is true.

In conclusion, only statements II and III are true.
