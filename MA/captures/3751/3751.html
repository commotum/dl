Example: Identifying Shapes: Rhombuses and Rectangles
?

Which of the shapes below is a rhombus?

EXPLANATION

Let's examine each of the shapes one by one:

  • Shape I is a rhombus. It has equal sides and pairs of parallel sides (but no right angle).

  • Shape II shows a trapezoid (not a rhombus) since it has sides but only pair of parallel sides.

  • Shape III represents a polygon with sides, so it's not a rhombus.

Therefore, the correct answer is "I only".
