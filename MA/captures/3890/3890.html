The place value chart for this number is as follows:

This place value chart has a ten-thousands column and a hundred-thousands column, as well as the usual thousands, hundreds, tens, and ones columns.

We say that has

  • hundred-thousands

  • ten-thousands

  • thousands

  • hundreds

  • tens, and

  • ones.
