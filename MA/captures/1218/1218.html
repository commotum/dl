Introduction
?

There are several quantities that businesses need to keep track of to ensure that the business remains healthy:

  • Revenue: This is the total amount of money (in dollars) that a company gains after selling units of product.

  • Cost: This is the total amount of money (in dollars) that a company spends to create units of product.

  • Profit. The difference between revenue and cost. It represents the amount of additional money that the business has earned after paying its costs.

Businesses often strive to maximize revenue and profit. They also often seek to minimize cost. The candidates test gives them a method for doing this. Let's find out how.
